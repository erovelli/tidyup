# Biology 201 — Lecture Notes: Cellular Respiration

Study notes from this week's lectures. Review before the midterm.

## Overview
Cellular respiration converts glucose into ATP across three stages.

## Glycolysis
- Occurs in the cytoplasm.
- One glucose yields two pyruvate, a net two ATP, and two NADH.

## Krebs Cycle
- Takes place in the mitochondrial matrix.
- Produces NADH and FADH2 used by the electron transport chain.

## Electron Transport Chain
- Located in the inner mitochondrial membrane.
- Oxygen is the final electron acceptor; produces the bulk of ATP.
